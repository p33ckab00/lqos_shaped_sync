Permission summary

Owner: users, updates, setup/repair, config, policies, backups, and live actions.

Admin: config, policies, scheduler, backup restore/delete, operations, and LibreQoS apply actions. Cannot manage users or Update Center.

Operator: can monitor, review reports/lifecycle/operations, and run dry-run style checks. Cannot change production config or destructive actions.

Viewer: read-only access for dashboards, docs, reports, devices, and status pages.
